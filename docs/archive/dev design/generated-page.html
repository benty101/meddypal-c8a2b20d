Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data really mine?

Yes. MeddyPal is a custodian, not an owner. You can export your full FHIR-compliant record or delete it permanently at any time.

Can I use MeddyPal without insurance?

Absolutely. You can use the health records, find cash-pay providers, and access telemedicine. If you want insurance later, you can compare plans in the marketplace.

What if I only have a feature phone?

We built MeddyPal for you. Use our USSD codes (*347#) and SMS services to book appointments, check insurance, and refill medications.

How secure is the genomics data?

Extremely secure. We use blockchain-backed consent and enterprise-grade encryption. Your genomic data is only shared with partners you explicitly authorize.
